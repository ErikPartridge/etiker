    Preamble

    Etiker provides ethics consulting for tech-related companies. We provide a website to facilitate communication with clients and prospective clients, as as to promote the Etiker brand. At Etiker, your privacy, comfort, and security is our priority. This policy explains how we process information that can be used either directly or indirectly to identify a user of our website ("Personal Data"), that is collected through this website.

    We believe all your personal data is confidential and inherently personal. We only use any data collected within Etiker, and will not share your information with other third parties, if required by law. If we are required by law to disclose information about you, we will take all steps that are permissible to notify you.
