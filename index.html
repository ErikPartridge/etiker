    What We Do

    We're here to help you with the ethical conundrums of the 21st century. In an increasingly connected and fast-paced world, ensuring ethicality can be tough, as hard as we try. We review your practices, from day-to-day operations to mission statement. We provide recommendations for you, and generate a report and, in some cases, a certification, as a result of our audit. This helps assure investors and customers alike that you're being as ethical as possible.

    Auditing

    We review your practices, code, and policies to ensure the ethical standards are met. We chat with your team members, look at data storage, and make an evaluation. This evaluation is based on societal norms, ethical baselines, and the principle of informed consent. Want to know more? We've got a whole page dedicated to this.

    Advising

    The time to tackle ethics is early on. Etiker can help you build an ethical product from the start. We'll be there with you as it progresses from idea, to MVP, to a top-seller. Got an established product? The time to grapple with the ethical questions is now, not in the media. Let's chat!

    Public Relations

    The time to address ethical challenges is before you're in the news. By tackling ethics early, we can help you avoid the negative spotlight. Plus, ethical practices can you help get good press in the tech world. Showing that you're dedicated to doing things right will help you draw attention for all the right reasons.


    Plus, having an audit on hand serves you well when you get asked about ethics. This is happening more and more often, so it's good to be prepared.

    Investor Confidence

    Investors can be sure that they're investing in an ethical company that won't cause them pain down the road. This can indicate to investors solid management and lower unexpected costs. Both positive signs for any company looking to raise funds.


    Ask about our one page review sheet, which serves as a brief and easily readable overview of your company's ethical strengths and weaknesses.

    User Retention

    User trust and user retention go hand in hand. Assuring users that you're using their data responsibly helps build the relationship. Then, continued diligence to ethical practices with frequent check-ups helps keep users around.


    When it comes to building a trustworthy brand ethics is a fundamental piece, and with our help, can be one of the easiest pieces.

    Employee Happiness

    Employees increasingly are motivated by a sense of doing good. Implementing ethical practices, and having these practices reviewed helps to show your team that their work matters. Plus, studies show that happier and more motivated employees are more productive.


    Recent news has demonstrated the relevance of ethics, as scores of employees have left major companies accused of ethics shortcomings. Ethical practices help to keep employees better motivated and more committed to your organization.
