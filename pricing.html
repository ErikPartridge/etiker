    • Our Pricing Model

    At Etiker, we work with each client to determine the best audit for their business model. We've listed some sample prices below for our auditing services. These are subject to change based on the size and needs of each company. We hope that these provide a useful frame of reference for you while you consider our services.


    Given the variable nature of ethics consulting, we have not listed any sample prices for that particular service. We look forward to working with you to craft a plan that works best for your company.


    Prices are especially variable based on the number of markets you wish to be evaluated for, the location of your business, the sector in which you work, and the number and complexity of your services. If additional discrimination review is required, that may also incur further costs.


    This isn't the cheapest service, and we understand that. However, we're confident that our service will pay off in reduced legal fees, increased investment and user engagement, and less need for public relations help. We're dedicated to being thorough. We won't certify any company unless we're 100% confident in their ethics, and as a result, that increases costs. Ultimately though, our attention to detail means greater value to you.
