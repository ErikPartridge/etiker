    Ethics Auditing

    Our ethics auditing is designed to thoroughly review your business' policies, procedures, and algorithms for ethical practices. We work with each business to ensure the extent, and fashion of the review is congruent with their business model and field.

    At the simplest, we take in a detailed write up of all your practices and a copy of your policies. Then, we thoroughly review the information you provide. We then follow up with you to request additional information as required, and issue a simple report outlining our evaluation of your business' ethics. In some cases, we may also be able to issue a certification following this review.

    In most cases, a simple document review is not the most effective approach.
