    • About Us

            Etiker (eh-tik-er) is a young, Ottawa, Ontario based company. Etiker aids tech corporations across the globe with their ethics needs. We exist because we believe that ethics are vital to the good function of our society. Furthermore, we believe that good ethics are beneficial to business and society alike. This belief is strongly supported by research, which demonstrates that upholding corporate social responsibility is among the top factors in long-term corporate profits.

    Our Principles

             Our approach to ethics begins with the principle of informed consent. This generally means that a user must be informed as to the use of their data, and consent to it being used in that way. However, this isn't always as simple as a checkbox. Societal values and norms play a large role in what level of informed consent is required. For example, tracking people using anonymized IP addresses when they visit blog requires a much lower level of consent than selling a user's social security number.

             Now, this is our approach to data ethics. However, not all ethics challenges are data challenges. We, in some cases, also look for instances of racism or other unwanted discrimination within a business. We also look broadly if non-data practices are ethical. Such practices may include marketing, recruiting, etc. However, we understand that the non-data practices are also subject to the importance of societal norms.

             Societal norms, of course, vary by country. What is considered acceptable in the United States may not be in the European Union and vice-versa. We don't standardize to any one country--we consult with experts in any and all markets that may be required. This is also what allows us to support ethics across the globe, without being locked in to North America.
